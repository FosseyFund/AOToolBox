Download and get started today!

User Friendly

Animal Observer is a free, easy-to-use iPad application for behavioral data collection, with a SIMPLE and straightforward interface developed to record data VERY FAST in the field or in captive settings. Perfect for ADVANCED research or student projects.

Entirely Customizable

This application is completely customizable for data collection on any observable species with discernible individuals. Add your study animals, pictures, social groups, habitat maps, behavior/activity variables, health variables and more!

Spatially Explicit

Using the GPS and compass features, Animal Observer records the latitude, longitude, altitude, and gps precision of every recorded data point. During scans, the app will also record the x,y coordinates of all the individuals on the scene and will an save the compass bearing of the focal individual.
